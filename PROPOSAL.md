# Mini Project 2: Proposal

## What I'm building

A daily schedule builder that takes a list of tasks with time estimates and priorities, then generates an organized time-blocked schedule for your day.

## Who it's for / Why

This is for anyone juggling multiple commitments and struggling to structure their day -- students balancing classes, projects, and work, or freelancers managing client deadlines alongside personal tasks. Instead of staring at a blank calendar trying to figure out what to do when, you dump everything in and get a structured plan back.

## Core features

1. Add tasks with a name, estimated duration, priority level (high/medium/low), and optional deadline
2. Generate a time-blocked daily schedule that organizes tasks by priority and fits them into available hours
3. Edit or remove tasks from the list before and after generating the schedule
4. Visual schedule view that displays your day as color-coded time blocks
5. Reset/clear everything to start planning a new day

## What I don't know yet

- How to implement drag-and-drop reordering in vanilla JavaScript (HTML Drag and Drop API)
- Best approach for the scheduling algorithm (sorting by priority vs. deadline vs. duration)
- How to use localStorage to persist the task list and generated schedule between sessions
- How to make time block rendering responsive and clean without a framework
